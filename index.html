Who Am I? 🤔

I’m a software engineer based in Montreal, Canada, with a curious mind that roams across Mathematics, Physics, Psychology, Philosophy, and Computer Science—and beyond. I’m a proud beginner in most of these fields; as the meme goes, You know nothing, Soheil Yousefi. That humility fuels my drive to learn continuously, ask “why,” and ship impactful code.
Trained in Aerospace Engineering at Iran’s top school, I pivoted into tech at Digikala, the country’s largest e-commerce platform. Starting as a Data Analyst, I rose to Reporting & Automation Supervisor, mastering Excel, Power BI, SQL, Python (pandas, automation), critical thinking, leadership, and cross-functional collaboration. While supervising, I self-studied design patterns, system architecture, and clean code, then—with colleagues’ mentorship—transitioned into a Software Engineer role within the same company.
In 2022, my wife and I moved to Canada. I joined SecureAuth (identity management), diving into cloud infrastructure (AWS), CI/CD, and MLOps—learning the real-world challenges of productionizing machine learning. Today, I’m on the AI team at UKG, a global HR platform leader serving lots of people around the globe. I help in building production-grade services like a RAG pipeline with LangChain + Google Gemini and an agent orchestration framework using LangGraph + LLMs, all running on Kubernetes.
I take ownership of every line of code, automate the tedious, and focus on impact. Always learning. Always shipping. Always iterating. Open to opportunities in AI systems, distributed architecture, or anything that pushes software’s boundaries.
